Lord Kalki

Kalki is the final incarnation of the Hindu god Vishnu. The term "avatara" means "descent" and refers to the divine's descent into the human realm. Kalki is mentioned in the ancient Hindu epic Mahabharata as the final earthly incarnation of Vishnu. He is also mentioned in other Hindu texts, including the Harivamsa Purana, Kalki Purana, Agni Purana, Brahmanda Purana, Bhavishyottara Purana, and Bhagavata Mahapurana.
